Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • chocolate chips
  • butter

Instructions

  1. mix ingredients
  2. preheat the oven to 350°F
  3. bake for 20 minutes
  4. let cool for 10 minutes and enjoy
